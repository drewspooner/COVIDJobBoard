How Does It Work

Job searches are often tedious and at times unnecessarily frustrating, and the COVID-19 outbreak has worsened the job search situation. The goal of this platform is to enable users to continue to search for jobs and internships, even during the chaos caused by COVID-19. A user will be able to search by location, filter out canceled internships, or even find remote positions.

Universal Accessibility

Our team believes that any user should be able to use our base site, regardless of ability. This is why we have implemented concepts of universal accessibility on this site. The aim is that users who are visually impaired can still have a positive experience on our site. This includes flexible website design that works even when a user needs to zoom in. Alt text is included wherever necessary to improve the experience for those using screenreaders. As well, all text fields are surrounded by backgrounds with maximum contrast to be color-blind friendly.
